# Code of Conduct

Adapted from Future Modern's Cooperative Covenant. See `src/app/policies/covenant/page.tsx` for the full text at production.

## What we ask of anyone in this repo

- **Deliver what you agreed to.** If you say you'll open a PR, open it. If you say you'll review, review. Communicate if plans change.
- **Communicate clearly.** English is the working language for issues and PRs. Explain what you did, why, and what tradeoffs you made.
- **Give honest review.** If a proposal has problems, say so directly. Softening critique to spare feelings devalues the review for everyone.
- **Respect confidentiality.** Some parts of the operating system reference internal governance (compliance penalties, comp structure, member tier decisions). If you encounter internal-facing content, don't broadcast it.
- **No harassment or discrimination.** The line is behavior, not identity. Everyone who follows the covenant is welcome.

## What earns a stronger response

The failure mode of pooled labor is not weak workers. It is strong workers who throw out the agreement when a check clears. The same principle applies here — technical talent doesn't excuse breaking the covenant.

- **Extraction posture.** Contributing solely to lift the code for a competing commercial fork violates the trademark and NOTICE terms, and — separately — is a values mismatch. We'll flag it.
- **Silent circumvention.** Attempting to route contributor discussions off-platform to bypass moderation.
- **Sustained bad-faith engagement.** Not disagreement or heat — those are fine. Sustained pattern of turning discussions unproductive after being asked to change.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close issues or PRs, or block contributors for violations. Serious or repeated violations get escalated to Future Modern's admin team.

The goal is to keep the space one where a thoughtful contributor wants to spend time. Not a space where everyone tolerates everyone. Discernment is a locked FM value; it applies here too.

## Reporting

Email `conduct@buildstore` (production) or the /contact form (sandbox). We'll acknowledge within 3 business days.
